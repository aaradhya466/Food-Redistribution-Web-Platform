Welcome to FoodBridge

Connecting Surplus Food with the Needy. Together, We Can Fight Hunger and Reduce Waste

About Us

FoodBridge is a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between surplus food and those in need. Our goal is to reduce food wastage and ensure that no one has to go hungry. We connect restaurants, cafes, event organizers, and other food donors with NGOs, volunteers, and people in need, allowing them to easily redistribute excess food.

We believe in a future where food insecurity is eradicated, and we work tirelessly to make that future a reality. Join us in our mission to make the world a better place by reducing food waste and helping those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: reduce food waste and alleviate hunger. We aim to connect individuals and organizations with excess food to those who can use it, creating a cycle of giving that benefits both communities and the environment.

How It Works

1. **Donors**: List surplus food available for donation.
2. **NGOs**: Browse and claim available donations.
3. **Volunteers**: Help facilitate the transportation and distribution of food.
